Common Questions

Honest answers about Lutron and Ketra.

Is Ketra tunable lighting worth the cost compared to standard LED?

For a Gold Coast estate, yes — by a significant margin. Ketra dims continuously to 0.1%, shifts from 1,400K to 10,000K without losing color accuracy, and monitors its own output 360 times per second. Fixtures will not 'popcorn' on at startup, and the light will look identical on day one and year ten. The fixture cost is higher, but it is a permanent architectural decision — one worth making once, correctly.

Can Ketra be added to a historic estate without opening finished ceilings?

Yes. Ketra's wireless architecture requires no new control wiring, and the recently introduced Ketra D2 Remodeler downlight is specifically designed to install through a finished ceiling — available in fixed, adjustable, and wall-wash configurations. For homes with an older Lutron HomeWorks system already installed, Lutron's legacy upgrade path allows access to the full Ketra portfolio without replacing the existing processor infrastructure.

What is Ketra's Natural Light feature and what does it actually do in daily use?

Ketra Natural Light shifts the color temperature and intensity of every fixture in a space gradually across the day, mirroring the arc of natural sunlight. Morning light is cooler and brighter. By evening it transitions to a warm, low amber that signals the body to wind down. For estate owners who spend extended time at home — or for properties with strong seasonal rhythms — the effect is a meaningful improvement in how comfortable the house feels throughout the day.

What is the difference between Ketra, Rania, and Lumaris?

Ketra is the flagship: the widest color temperature range, the most precise color lock, and the deepest dimming. It belongs in primary living spaces, art collections, and anywhere the quality of light is noticed. Rania offers a refined architectural profile with a slightly narrower performance band — excellent for secondary spaces. Lumaris covers warm-dim and tunable applications for streamlined integration. HTE specifies this decision room by room during the design phase.

Does Ketra work with my existing Lutron system, or do I need HomeWorks?

Ketra integrates with Lutron HomeWorks QS and does not currently integrate directly with RadioRA 2. If your home has an existing HomeWorks QS system, adding Ketra is often straightforward. If you have an older HomeWorks platform, Lutron's legacy upgrade path may allow Ketra access without a full replacement. HTE assesses your existing infrastructure as part of every consultation.

How does Ketra protect fine art and interior finishes over time?

Ketra's Color Lock technology monitors light output 360 times per second, maintaining consistent, accurate color rendering for the life of the system — preventing the gradual color drift that causes standard LEDs to misrepresent finishes and artwork as they age. Ketra Vibrancy adds a third layer of control, allowing the recipe of white light to be adjusted so that art and textiles render with more depth and presence. Institutions including the Art Institute of Chicago use the same technology for precisely this reason.
