Common Questions

Honest answers about Gold Coast smart home installation.

What does smart home installation cost in a Gold Coast Long Island estate?

Scope drives cost more than anything else. A Lutron RadioRA 3 system with Sonos, motorized shades, and mesh Wi-Fi for a Great Neck colonial starts in the mid-five figures. A full Gold Coast estate configuration — HomeWorks QSX, Control4 or Crestron, Palladiom shading, distributed audio, pool/spa control, gate intercom, a dedicated theater, and Cat6A infrastructure across the main house and outbuildings — represents a meaningful six-figure investment. During your consultation we outline scope, phasing options, and what a realistic long-term budget looks like for your specific property.

Can you install smart home technology in a historic Gold Coast estate without major renovation?

Yes — and it's one of the most common projects we do on the North Shore. Lutron RadioRA 3's wireless architecture eliminates the need to run new wire through plaster or brick walls. Architectural in-ceiling speakers integrate invisibly into existing ceilings. A structured network overlay can modernize a 1920s Tudor or Georgian estate without disturbing a single original detail. We assess each home's construction before recommending an approach.

What is the difference between Control4 X4 and Crestron Home — and which fits my home?

We think of it this way: Control4 for confidence, Crestron for customization. Control4 X4 now lets homeowners modify scenes and schedules directly from the app — no integrator call needed for everyday adjustments. It also integrates with Apple HomeKit across iPhone, CarPlay, and Mac, which matters for commuters. Crestron Home OS 4 offers more granular programming and suits estates with highly bespoke requirements. We're platform-agnostic — we recommend what fits your home, not what's simplest for us.

What is the difference between Lutron HomeWorks QSX and Lutron RadioRA 3?

HomeWorks QSX is Lutron's flagship panelized system for large estates — it supports thousands of devices, Palladiom shading, Ketra tunable lighting, and enterprise-grade reliability across main house and outbuildings. RadioRA 3 is wireless and retrofit-friendly, ideal for finished homes where running new conduit would be disruptive or destructive. Both integrate with Control4 and Crestron. For most Gold Coast new builds, HomeWorks QSX is the appropriate baseline. For finished historic homes, RadioRA 3 frequently delivers the same result.

How do I monitor my North Shore home remotely — especially when traveling or spending time in Florida?

Remote monitoring is one of the most common priorities we design for in this market. Our ExpertCare program provides 24/7 system monitoring, freeze alerts, water leak detection, automated light schedules that simulate occupancy, and a dedicated service manager who knows your property. Control4 X4's app lets you check status and adjust scenes from anywhere. We also design for cellular-backup alarm connectivity so your system stays online even during a network interruption.

Do you work on multi-building Gold Coast estates — pool houses, carriage houses, garages?

Multi-building estates are our standard operating environment on the Gold Coast. We design unified networks extending across the main residence, pool house, carriage house, detached garage, and tennis pavilion — one system, one app. Outdoor audio, weatherproof Wi-Fi access points, gate intercoms, and pool/spa control are all coordinated from a single platform. We manage cross-trade coordination with your architect, builder, and landscape contractor so nothing falls between trades.
